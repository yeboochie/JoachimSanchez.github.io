Who am I?

I am a Filipino graduate of Information Technology specializing in Digital Arts in FEU Institute of Technology. I'm passionate about combining creativity and technology to create innovative, appealing, and meaningful projects.


Throughout my academic journey, from as early as junior high school all the way to college, I continually learned about coding with various programming languages and IDEs, as well as the different forms and mediums of art. As my tertiary studies progressed, I learned full-stack development by starting with front-end web development and later transitioning to back-end web development. Additionally, I acquired and honed my skills in using digital platforms to create graphical representations of information and ideas, such as digital art illustration, graphic designing and video editing.


So I asked myself, what if I could assist or create websites or apps that would still allow me to apply my technological knowledge while focusing more on the artistic aspect? Fortunately, the UI/UX design and front-end web development career paths exist, and it's something I'm eager to pursue and improve upon.


Best of both worlds.
